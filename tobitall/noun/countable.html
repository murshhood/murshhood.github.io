Parts of Speech

NOUN

Countable Noun

The nouns that can be counted are called countable nouns. Countable nouns can take an article: a, an, the.

Examples in words:

  1. Chair
  2. Table
  3. Bat
  4. Ball
  5. Water
  6. Money
  7. Sugar
You can say 1 chair, 2 chairs, 3 chairs.

Examples in sentences:

  1. I bought a book of neurology.
  2. The book has 269 pages in it.
  3. It has 39 chapters in it.
  4. Each chapter covers 5 topics.
  5. Alex has four pencils.
